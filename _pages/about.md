My work spans the full stack of robotic systems, from low-level hardware integration and real-time communication to perception pipelines, state estimation, and algorithmic design.

I am motivated by problems that require both theoretical rigor and practical engineering, particularly in autonomous systems operating in complex, uncertain environments.
